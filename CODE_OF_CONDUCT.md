# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a respectful, harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on technical content, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and correct them quickly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Deliberate disruption of project collaboration.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block contributors for violations.

## Reporting

Open a private issue or contact maintainers through repository channels with details of the incident.
